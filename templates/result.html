What This Means for You
{% if dominant == 'Visual' %}

As a visual learner, you process information best when you can see it. You benefit from:

  • Diagrams, charts, and graphs
  • Color-coding and highlighting
  • Mind maps and visual organizers
  • Written instructions and notes
  • Flashcards with images
{% elif dominant == 'Auditory' %}

As an auditory learner, you process information best when you can hear it. You benefit from:

  • Listening to lectures and discussions
  • Reading aloud or using audiobooks
  • Participating in group discussions
  • Using mnemonics and rhymes
  • Recording and replaying lessons
{% else %}

As a kinesthetic learner, you process information best through movement and hands-on activities. You benefit from:

  • Hands-on experiments and activities
  • Taking breaks to move around
  • Using manipulatives and models
  • Writing notes by hand
  • Role-playing and simulations
{% endif %}
